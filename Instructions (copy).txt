Instructions
	This is march madness Simulation game based on College basketball play off across four divisions.
	You choose which team you think will win based on their stats.
	You can view their stats by hovering over the team and pushing the icon.
	Go through each bracket and select each team and then make your final choice in the final bracket.
	Once you are done you can have a friend login and make selection or simulate the game yourself.
	You will receive point for correct guesses and each user will be displayed from low to high.
